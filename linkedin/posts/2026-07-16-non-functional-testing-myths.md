# 3 non-functional testing myths, busted

## Hook (first 2–3 lines)

"It works" isn't the same as "it works under load, under attack, and at 2 a.m."

Non-functional testing is where quality quietly makes or breaks your release. Three myths we hear all the time 👇

## Body

Myth 1: "Performance testing is only for launch day."
Reality: load, stress, and soak tests belong in your pipeline — not the week before go-live.

Myth 2: "Security is the security team's problem."
Reality: reliability, performance, and security are quality attributes. They're everyone's job, and they're testable.

Myth 3: "Non-functional testing slows delivery down."
Reality: catching a scalability issue in CI is minutes. Catching it in production is a weekend.

At Qnit, non-functional testing is core to how we help teams ship cloud native and bespoke applications with confidence.

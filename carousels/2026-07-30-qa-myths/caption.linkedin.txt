"It works" isn't the same as "it works under load, under attack, and at 2 a.m." 🚀

Three non-functional testing myths we hear all the time — and what's actually true:

1. "Performance testing is only for launch day."
Load, stress and soak tests belong in your pipeline, not the week before go-live.

2. "Security is the security team's problem."
Reliability, performance and security are quality attributes. They're everyone's job — and testable.

3. "Non-functional testing slows delivery down."
Catching a scalability issue in CI takes minutes. Catching it in production takes a weekend.

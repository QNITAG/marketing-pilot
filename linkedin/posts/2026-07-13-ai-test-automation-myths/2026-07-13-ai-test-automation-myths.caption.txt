AI can accelerate test automation.
It does not make test strategy optional.

Five myths worth challenging before teams scale AI-assisted testing:

Myth 1: "AI will replace testers."
Reality: AI can suggest tests and code. People still define intent, assess risk, and review the output.

Myth 2: "AI-generated tests are automatically correct and complete."
Reality: generated tests can miss scenarios, and generated code still needs validation.

Myth 3: "With AI, every test should be automated."
Reality: teams still need to choose the right test level and decide when manual testing is more appropriate.

Myth 4: "AI eliminates flaky tests and maintenance."
Reality: scope, synchronization, isolation, test data, and architecture still shape reliability.

Myth 5: "Governance can come later."
Reality: responsible adoption means managing trustworthiness and risk across the AI lifecycle.

AI can be a useful accelerator. The quality of the outcome still depends on the engineering decisions around it.

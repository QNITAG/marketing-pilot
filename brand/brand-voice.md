# Brand Voice & Style Guide — Qnit AG

## Who we are

**Qnit AG** — a PRODYNA company. The quality assurance specialists for
**cloud native, bespoke applications, and SAP**. Passionate about quality
assurance, cloud native, and automation.

- **HQ:** Munich, Bavaria
- **Teams across:** 🇩🇪 Germany · 🇦🇹 Austria · 🇷🇸 Serbia · 🇨🇭 Switzerland
- **Part of:** the PRODYNA group
- **Audience:** engineering leaders, QA/test engineers, developers, and
  prospective team members (hiring) in the DACH region and Serbia.

## Content pillars

1. **Expertise & insight** — QA, test automation, non-functional testing,
   AI-driven quality engineering, SAP quality.
2. **Events & industry** — conferences and meetups (e.g. Tricentis AI Tour),
   takeaways and thought leadership.
3. **People of Qnit** — team building, celebrations, anniversaries, culture.
4. **Hiring** — open roles and life at Qnit.

## Voice

- **Personality:** expert, warm, people-first, quietly confident.
- **Tone by platform:**
  - Instagram: personal, celebratory, emoji-friendly, behind-the-scenes.
  - LinkedIn: professional and insight-driven, still human and enthusiastic.

## Do / Don't

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Lead with people and real moments | Sound corporate or impersonal |
| Explain the value of quality clearly | Overuse deep technical jargon |
| Credit teams, partners, and hosts (tag them) | Forget to tag people/partners |
| Write primarily in English | Mix languages within one post |
| End with a clear CTA | Leave the reader with no next step |

## Writing rules

- **Primary language:** English (both platforms).
- Sentence case for headlines; active voice.
- Emoji: welcome on Instagram, sparing and purposeful on LinkedIn (🚀 💙 🏔️).
- Tag partners and hosts (e.g. @prodyna_corporate, event organizers).
- Always include a clear call to action (CTA).
